about

hi! my name is jade and i'm a front-end developer from toronto. before i was a dev, i was getting my bachelor's of arts at the university of toronto but decided to pursue coding as it's something that's always been sitting in the back of my mind. i enjoy the aspects and intersections between design and functionality and being able to not only formulate and build upon an idea, but also having the capability to make it happen. when i'm not bashing keys, you can find me sleeping, napping, or under a blanket. now that i'm done bootcamp, i'm excited to have the time to review, refine and build upon my coding knowledge and return to the task of petting all the dogs in the city.
